Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Fostering a tech driven economy through research, entrepreneurship, and digital, empowering industries with cutting edge solutions for sustainable growth.

Sustainability

Prioritizing eco friendly policies, renewable energy, and responsible resource management to create a greener, healthier future for generations to come.

Infrastructure

Building smart cities, modern transportation, and robust digital connectivity to support economic growth and improve citizens' quality of life.

Empowerment

Enhancing education, skill development, and social equity to uplift communities, ensuring equal opportunities for all in a progressive Bangladesh.
